United City FC

About the Club

United City FC is a professional football club dedicated to excellence, teamwork, and community. Founded in 1998, we have been a cornerstone of local football for over 25 years.

We bring players and supporters together with programs that celebrate growth, respect, and determination.

Our club works with local schools and sponsors to build strong relationships in the city.

As a football club, our goal is to create opportunities for all ages to learn the game and enjoy team success.

We are committed to developing a culture of hard work, resilience, and pride in every training session and game.
